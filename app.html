Chai Chee Trail (Android)

Project Manager - October 2014 to January 2015

Designer - 2014 to 2015

The Chai Chee Trail Android App is a heritage walking guide deploying GPS technology. Besides providing historical and cultural information in multimedia format about the heritage sites it also educates on Singapore's unique racial harmony practices through interactive games embedded in the app. The app was launched by the Minister of Culture and Social Development during the Racial Harmony Day commemoration at Ping Yi Secondary School. The app made us Bronze Winner of SITF awards in Best Innovative ICM Student Project (Tertiary).

Took on the role of designer for the map menu of the app and artwork for the SATA Germ, Cathedral Car and Al Ansar Mosque Puzzle 2D games. Also designed the infographic for the app which was used during the launch of the app.

Owl Nation (Android)

Designer - 2014

Owl Nation is a game that encourages interaction among players in real life. Players are owls and work together to find the suspicious 'traitor' among the group. For every game, each player is assigned the same password except for the 'traitor'. On a per-round basis, players are to describe their own password and vote out who they think the player with the odd password is.

Along with the another designer, I had contributed to create the game graphics. I conceptualized the UI and overall look and feel, including colors, fonts and layout. I had also created the logo, icon and the iconic owl that is used throughout the game.
